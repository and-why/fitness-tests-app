AFA Fitness Score

Scores Explained

Scores are based on normative data collected from numerous studies. They show levels from very poor up to excellent. However, because these numbers are from a normative sample, they will not include highley trained athletes, and only a few will have acheived excellent scores.

If you're getting 20s, then you are probably well trained and very fit, but that doens't mean you're perfect.
